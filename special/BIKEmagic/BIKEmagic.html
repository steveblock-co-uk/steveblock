Hang on tight

Our previous high-altitude pic has attracted the attention of, to be totally frank, some authentic nutjobs. This is Bart Hallmark at 2,600m in the Grand Paradisio National Park in NW Italy. Him and Steve Block (who took the pic) were heading for France by way of 'via ferrata', a network of ladders and cables installed through the mountains many years ago to help the army move around otherwise inaccessible areas. Only they weren't carrying bikes...

We hardly dare ask if anyone's got pictures of riders in even more precariously exposed locations, but if you have we'd love to see them...
