In February 2007 I completed a Master of Science in Computer Science at MIT.

I worked in the Model-Based Embedded and Robotic Systems group with Prof. Brian Williams. My thesis, Distributed Method Selection and Dispatching of Contingent, Temporally Flexible Plans, developed an algorithm for reliably executing plans on groups of robots.

I published a number of papers on the technical content of my thesis, which are listed below.

  • ICAPS 2006 (Accepted for Planning under Uncertainty workshop only), Distributed Method Selection and Dispatching of Contingent, Temporally Flexible Plans
